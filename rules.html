RULES

  1. Cars can only be imported to India from any country
  2. The speedometer must display km/h in India as per the metric system
  3. The imported car must only be right-hand-drive
  4. The vehicle must not be older than three years
  5. The motor vehicle must be in compliance with the Motor Vehicle Act of 1988
  6. RTO will only register your vehicle after the completion of all the rules
  7. Initially, you will be given a temporary registration number and after 7 days
    a permanent number can be applied for.
